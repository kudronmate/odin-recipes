Greek Salad

Ingredients
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Feta Cheese
  • Onions
  • Green olives
  • Black olives
  • Oregano
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  1. Cut 4-5 normal sized tomatoes, 1 cucumber, 200 gramms of Feta cheese, 2 onions, some green and black olives!
  2. Pour some olive oil in a small cup, give some lemon jouice and oregano to it, and stir it thoroughly!
  3. Pour that olive oil mix on the salad, and stir it, give it an hour in the fridge, and then enjoy! :)
